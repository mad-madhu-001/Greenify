Together we can reduce Carbon footprint

A Carbon Footprint Calculator is a tool designed to estimate the total amount of greenhouse gases (GHGs) that individuals, households, or businesses emit into the atmosphere. It does this by analyzing various activities and behaviors, such as transportation, energy consumption, and waste production. The calculator then provides an estimate of the carbon emissions associated with these activities.

If you drive a car for 100 miles per week and consume 300 kWh of electricity per month, the calculator will estimate the amount of CO2 emissions generated from these activities. It might suggest switching to public transport or adopting energy-efficient appliances as ways to reduce your carbon footprint.
